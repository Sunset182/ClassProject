How to Make Spiced Pecan Hot Cocoa

A fun fall weather drink that everyone in the family will enjoy!

Ingredients

Amount Ingredients
1 Cup Coconut Milk
1/2 Cup Raw Pecans
2 tsp Pure Cocoa Powder
2 tsp Saigon Cinnamon (or any on hand)
1 tsp Madagascar Vanilla
1 tsp Bourbon Black Walnut Sugar
1 tsp Spiced Cocoa Mix
1/4 tsp Tellicherry Black Pepper

*Note: Double the recipe to serve 2 people!
